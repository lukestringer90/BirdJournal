Conservation status

Least Concern (IUCN 3.1)[1]
Binomial name
Lymnocryptes minimus
(Brunnich, 1764)

The Jack Snipe, Lymnocryptes minimus is a small stocky wader. It is the smallest snipe, and the only member of the genus Lymnocryptes. Features such as its sternum[2] make it quite distinct from other snipes or woodcocks.[3]
