The Caspian Tern (Hydroprogne caspia, formerly Sterna caspia;[1] syn. Hydroprogne tschegrava) is a species of tern, with a subcosmopolitan but scattered distribution. Despite its extensive range, it is monotypic of its genus, and has no subspecies accepted either.[2] In New Zealand it is also known by the Maori name Taranui.
