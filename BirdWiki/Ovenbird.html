The Ovenbird, Seiurus aurocapillus, is a small songbird of the New World warbler family (Parulidae). This migratory bird breeds in eastern North America and moves south in winter.[1]

It is the subject of a poem by Robert Frost, "The Oven Bird", published in his poetry collection Mountain Interval in 1916.
